Delete Your TimeMaster Account

If you wish to delete your TimeMaster account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to our support team at tomiokaisbuffed@gmail.com.
  2. Include your account username or email address in the message so we can locate your account.
  3. Request that your account and all associated data be deleted.

Data That Will Be Deleted

Upon deletion, we will remove the following:

  • All tasks and schedules associated with your account.
  • Your account credentials in Firebase Authentication.
  • Any personal information you provided within the app.

Data That May Be Retained

Some anonymized app usage analytics may be retained for performance monitoring or research purposes, but they will not be linked to your identity.

Timeframe

We will process your deletion request within 30 days of receiving your email.

Thank you for using TimeMaster.
